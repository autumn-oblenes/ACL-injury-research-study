About the Research

Understanding the biomechanical impact of footwear on high-intensity sports.

⚽

The Problem

ACL tears remain one of the most significant injuries in soccer. While training methods have evolved, the relationship with modern footwear remains under-studied.

🧪

Our Goal

To identify if specific stud patterns (FG vs AG) or shoe brands correlate with higher injury rates across different playing levels.

📊

Why Participate

Your data contributes to a safer future for soccer players at all levels, from grassroots to professional ranks.
